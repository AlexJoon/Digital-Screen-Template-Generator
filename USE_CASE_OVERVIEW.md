# Campus Event Slide Generator - Use Case Overview

## Target Users

**Primary Users:**
- Staff at Campus Centers (Student Center, Recreation Center, etc.)
- Program Office Staff (Academic programs, student services)
- Event Coordinators
- Department Administrators
- Student Affairs Staff

**User Profile:**
- Busy staff members who need to promote events quickly
- May not have design skills or PowerPoint expertise
- Need consistent, professional-looking event slides
- Work with tight deadlines
- Manage multiple events simultaneously

---

## The Problem We Solve

### Before This Tool:

**Staff faced these challenges:**

1. **Time-Consuming Design Work**
   - Had to manually create PowerPoint slides
   - Struggled with formatting and layout
   - Spent hours on what should be a quick task

2. **Inconsistent Branding**
   - Different staff used different templates
   - Campus branding often applied incorrectly
   - Slides looked unprofessional or off-brand

3. **Poor Digital Screen Optimization**
   - Slides often had too much text
   - Fonts too small to read from distance
   - Wrong aspect ratios for digital displays
   - Low contrast made viewing difficult

4. **Technical Barriers**
   - Not everyone comfortable with PowerPoint
   - Templates were complex to use
   - File compatibility issues

5. **Workflow Inefficiency**
   - Content existed in various formats (PDFs, emails, documents)
   - Had to manually copy/paste into slides
   - Lots of back-and-forth revisions

---

## The Solution

### With This Tool:

**Staff can now:**

1. **Upload Existing Content**
   - Use event flyers they already created
   - Upload word documents with event details
   - Convert PDFs into slides automatically

2. **Get Optimized Slides in Minutes**
   - 1-2 minute processing time
   - AI formats content for digital screens
   - Automatically applies campus branding

3. **Display Professional Results**
   - Large, readable text
   - High contrast design
   - Perfect 16:9 format for digital screens
   - Consistent campus branding

---

## Workflow Comparison

### Old Process (30-60 minutes)

```
1. Open PowerPoint template (5 min)
   ↓
2. Copy event information from various sources (10 min)
   ↓
3. Format text, adjust sizes, pick colors (15 min)
   ↓
4. Find and insert relevant images (10 min)
   ↓
5. Apply campus branding (logo, fonts) (5 min)
   ↓
6. Review and adjust for readability (10 min)
   ↓
7. Save and upload to digital signage (5 min)
   ↓
TOTAL: 30-60 minutes per event
```

### New Process (2-3 minutes)

```
1. Upload event content file (30 sec)
   ↓
2. Click "Generate Event Slides" (5 sec)
   ↓
3. Wait for processing (1-2 min)
   ↓
4. Download and upload to signage (30 sec)
   ↓
TOTAL: 2-3 minutes per event
```

**Time Savings: 90-95%**

## Typical Use Cases

### Use Case 1: Weekly Event Series

**Scenario:**
Student Activities Office runs weekly movie nights and needs slides each week.

**Process:**
1. Staff member has Word doc with movie title, date, time, location
2. Uploads document to tool
3. Generates 3 slides:
   - Slide 1: Movie title and tagline
   - Slide 2: Date, time, location, admission info
   - Slide 3: What to bring, parking info, next week's preview
4. Downloads and uploads to digital screens in Student Union
5. Repeats each week in 2 minutes

**Result:** 52 events/year created in 104 minutes instead of 26-52 hours

---

### Use Case 2: Guest Speaker Event

**Scenario:**
Academic department hosting visiting professor lecture.

**Process:**
1. Department has PDF flyer with speaker bio and lecture details
2. Uploads PDF to tool
3. Selects "Professional" tone
4. Adds custom detail: "Light refreshments provided"
5. Generates 3 slides:
   - Slide 1: Speaker name and credentials
   - Slide 2: Lecture topic, date, time, location
   - Slide 3: Key themes, Q&A info, refreshments
6. Downloads for display in academic buildings

**Result:** Professional slides matching flyer, created in minutes

---

### Use Case 3: Multi-Location Event

**Scenario:**
Campus-wide orientation with sessions in different buildings.

**Process:**
1. Orientation team has master schedule document
2. Uploads schedule
3. Adds custom details for specific location
4. Generates slides for each location
5. Distributes to digital screens in all buildings

**Result:** Consistent messaging across all locations, quickly produced

---

### Use Case 4: Last-Minute Event Changes

**Scenario:**
Weather forces indoor venue change 24 hours before event.

**Process:**
1. Staff retrieves original event document
2. Updates location information
3. Re-uploads to tool
4. Adds custom instruction: "LOCATION CHANGE - NOW INDOORS"
5. Generates new slides with updated location
6. Quickly replaces old slides on digital screens

**Result:** Fast response to changes, all screens updated within minutes

## Digital Screen Display Strategy

### The 3-Slide Format

**Slide 1: Event Title & Hook (3-5 seconds display)**
```
Purpose: Grab attention as people walk by
Content:
- Large event title
- Compelling tagline or benefit
- Eye-catching visual (if enabled)

Example:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
  SPRING CAREER FAIR
  Connect with 50+ Employers
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
```

**Slide 2: Essential Details (5-7 seconds display)**
```
Purpose: Provide critical event information
Content:
- Date and time (large, bold)
- Location with building/room
- Registration/admission info

Example:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Thursday, March 15, 2024
10:00 AM - 3:00 PM

Student Union
Grand Ballroom

Free & Open to All Students
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
```

**Slide 3: Highlights & Call to Action (3-5 seconds display)**
```
Purpose: Motivate attendance and action
Content:
- Key features or benefits
- What to bring/expect
- How to register or learn more

Example:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
✓ On-the-spot interviews
✓ Resume review station
✓ Professional headshots
✓ Networking workshops

Register: careers.campus.edu/fair
Bring: Resumes & Business Attire
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
```

**Total Loop Time: 11-17 seconds**
Perfect for repeating rotation with other campus announcements

## Content Guidelines for Staff

### What Makes Good Source Content

**Include:**
- Clear event title
- Specific date and time
- Complete location (building + room)
- Brief description (2-3 sentences)
- Key highlights (3-5 bullet points)
- Registration/RSVP info
- Contact for questions

**Avoid:**
- Excessive detail (AI will condense)
- Vague time references ("next Tuesday")
- Missing critical info
- Multiple events in one document

### Sample Good Source Document

```
MENTAL HEALTH AWARENESS WORKSHOP
Supporting Students in Crisis

DATE: Wednesday, April 10, 2024
TIME: 2:00 PM - 4:00 PM
LOCATION: Wellness Center, Training Room A

Learn to recognize signs of mental health crisis and
connect students with appropriate resources. This
interactive workshop covers active listening, de-escalation
techniques, and campus support services.

HIGHLIGHTS:
- Practical communication strategies
- Resource guide provided
- Continuing education credits available
- Small group practice sessions

REGISTRATION REQUIRED
wellness.campus.edu/workshops
Limited to 25 participants

QUESTIONS: wellness@campus.edu
```
