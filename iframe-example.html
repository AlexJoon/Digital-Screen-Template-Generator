Usage Guide for Staff

Step 1: Upload

Upload your event flyer, document, or content file (PDF, Word, etc.)

Step 2: Review

Optionally adjust tone and add specific event details

Step 3: Generate

Click generate and wait 1-2 minutes for processing

Step 4: Download

Download your PowerPoint and upload to digital signage system

Note: Slides are formatted in 16:9 widescreen for standard digital displays. For best results, include event title, date/time, location, and key highlights in your upload.
